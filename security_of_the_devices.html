Problems:

  • Reveal personal information.
  • Stolen important data.
  • Stolen money from bank account through internet banking in mobile.
  • Store valuable data and information.

Solutions:

  • Use strong passwords.
  • Unsure public or free wifi is protected.
  • Utilize VPN.
  • Encrypt your device.
  • Install an antivirus application.
  • Update to the lasted software.
  • Log out everytime shut down device.
  • Use only trusted stores.
