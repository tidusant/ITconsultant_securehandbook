Security during use (of systems, computers…)

Problems:

  • Install security software from reliable companies and keep updating this software
  • Beware with email attached files, because the hacker can send you attractive email that have malware within. These attached files can look like normal file such as pdf, docx, mp3, etc.
  • Access to public wifi and visit on some untrusted website (weird or illegal domains, domain without https)
  • Fill some private information to form in un-reliable website for registering, logging or applying.
  • Download free resources which have malware inside.
  • Download low reliable, free anti-virus software. Anti-virus software requires full access to your computer. Therefore, installing such anti-software can increase the level of risk.
  • Turn off anti-virus software, firewall.
  • Internal employees leak private company information via email, message apps or social media.
  • Use predictable and weak password (contain name, birthday or anything relate to your public information) for some critical account such as bank account, email, company account etc.
  • Only use one password for all.

Precaution:

  • Keep the software up-to-date
  • Regularly scan your computer and backup your files and remove trash in computer.
  • Change importance accounts ‘s password each quite long period time (every 6 months).
  • Always turn on anti-virus, firewall.
  • In company network, Block any files would be sent via email and social media (block message, block upload file).
  • Block and do not attempt to visit untrusted website.
  • Never click attached files from unknown emails.
  • Do not download free + crack software.
  • Never save your username + password of importance account (bank account, company account, …) in browser or other devices.
  • Use virtual keyboard when typing password.
